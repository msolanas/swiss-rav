# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Swiss RAV Assistant to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone, regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, religion, or sexual identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that helps:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for users (Swiss job seekers managing real, stressful RAV obligations)

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information (including any real RAV/ALK/personal data) without permission
- Other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Privacy note specific to this project

This plugin handles sensitive personal data (employment, benefits, health, identity). **Never paste real personal data — yours or anyone else's — into issues, PRs, or discussions.** Redact names, addresses, IBANs, AHV numbers, and case references when reporting bugs.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behaviour by opening a confidential channel via the repository maintainer (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for private contact). Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this code, and may ban contributors for serious or repeated violations.
